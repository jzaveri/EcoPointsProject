About This Project

EcoTracker is a platform designed to make sustainability fun, rewarding, and community-driven. We believe that small everyday actions—like bringing a reusable water bottle, biking instead of driving, or supporting local eco-conscious businesses—can add up to a big impact when tracked, shared, and celebrated.

Our mission is to encourage individuals to build eco-friendly habits by earning points for sustainable actions. These points unlock badges, and even achievements.

We’re combining the power of gamification, community engagement, and real-world rewards to spark long-term behavior change. By turning sustainability into a fun, social, and trackable journey, we hope to raise environmental awareness while directly supporting local ecosystems—both ecological and economic.

Whether you're a seasoned environmentalist or just getting started, our platform welcomes everyone to join the movement and take small steps toward a more sustainable lifestyle. Together, we can turn good habits into lasting impact.
